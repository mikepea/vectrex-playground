Sending Feedback


In creating this bit of Vectrex history, I'm trying to organize and present various notes and pieces of source code that were written over 30 years ago. While my notes were more detailed than I remembered, it's obvious that some of my notes and related documents appear to have been lost.

While I want to thank you for taking the time to write, there are a couple of points that I would like you to keep in mind:

- I tried to be as careful as I could, but there are probably errors. Should you find an error or have additional information, please let me know. Although I'll try to respond quickly, but our Gazetteer takes priority and it's likely that I'm going to be slow in responding.

- We have already placed what information that we kind find on our website. Over time we might find more, but it seems unlikely.

- When writing to RoadsideThoughts, please remember that administration of the website is done as time permits. We start every day with good intentions, but there are times when we get overwhelmed and it will take a while before can we get caught-up.

- Any E-Mail sent with a blank Subject Line will be discarded without opening.
